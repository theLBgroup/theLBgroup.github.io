Our group is part of the Department of Chemical Engineering, the Manufacturing Futures Lab (MFL), and the Centre for Nature-Inspired Engineering (CNIE). We aim at designing the next generation of green functional materials that are relevant to global challenges like healthcare, renewable energy, or the environment. We strive to develop these materials entirely from renewable resources, to ensure the circularity of their manufacturing. Our research focuses on biopolymers, sitting at the intersection between chemical engineering, materials science, and computational modelling.
