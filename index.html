Structural proteins for biomedical materials

The SUPERB project (StructUral ProtEins foR Biomedical materials), funded as a H2020 Marie Curie Fellowship, deals with the experimental and computational design of protein-based biomedical materials.
Updates on scientific progress and publications, outreach activities and computational codes, are regularly posted here.

  • Learn More
My research work combines experimental and computational approaches for the development of advanced multifunctional materials entirely made of renewable bioresources.

Research areas:

  • Biobased/Biopolymeric materials
  • Computational modelling
  • Process engineering
