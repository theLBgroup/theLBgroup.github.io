We aim to design the next generation of soft functional materials to address grand challenges in healthcare, infrastructure, or the environment. We also strive to develop these materials entirely from renewable resources, ensuring the circularity of their manufacturing. Our research combines experimental and computational tools at the intersection between (bio)chemical engineering, materials science, and computational modelling. We are part of the Department of Chemical Engineering, the Manufacturing Futures Lab (MFL), and the Centre for Nature-Inspired Engineering (CNIE).
